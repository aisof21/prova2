ABOUT

LINN FRITZ

Linn Fritz is a Swedish illustrator, designer, and animator who lives in London. She
creates precise and clean images in pastel tones for editorial and commercial clients.
She honed her fluid line work during her time at Hyper Island, where she learned to
draw digitally to have more creative freedom and room for mistakes. Linn is also a
co-founder of Panimation, a community that supports women, trans, and non-binary
individuals in the animation industry. Her favorite projects revolve around feminism,
clothing, plants, and interior design, but she also appreciates clients who offer a good
collaborative relationship based on trust and creative freedom.
