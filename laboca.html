ABOUT

LA BOCA

La Boca is a design studio based in west London, founded in 2002, that has gained
recognition for their ability to create album sleeves with a retro feel. Their psychedelic
style is a combination of each team member's unique influences, and they have won
numerous awards such as Cannes Lions and European Design Awards. With
members from London, Bordeaux, Devon, and Switzerland, the team is always busy
with work from dedicated clients and is eager to take on more animated projects in
the future. La Boca operates from two studios, one in London and one in Amsterdam.
