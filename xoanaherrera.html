ABOUT

XOANA HERRERA

The portfolio of LA-based illustrator Xoana Herrera is an explosion of sumptuous, stylish
character scenes dwelling in a world that, frankly, looks ideal. Xoana trained as a
graphic designer in Argentina but over time found herself drawn to the world of
illustration after a five-year stint at Buck agency. Now, alongside founding a feminist
creative collective, Get it Girl, she creates graceful, complex and accessible imagery
which would be as at home in an ad campaign as it would be in a children’s book
(which she's quite keen to try soon).
